Damascus Waters

Damascus Waters (occasionally known as Dami Waters) is the pseudonym of a New England savant. This name has been used socially within academic circles, as it is more suitable than their birthname.

Damascus prides themself on thier aesthetics and ambition, especially in terms of continuous self-improvement.

Early Life

Born in Western Massachusetts, Damascus began their life as one of humble means. Though by providence established as middle class, Damascus dreamed of financial and professional influence. Luck, they decided, was not the way to reach these goals. They must be achieved, rather, by means of hard work and perseverence
